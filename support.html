Daybook

Support

Daybook is a personal planner for tasks, events, reminders, and quick journal captures, made by NovaWealth. If something isn't working or you have a question, we'd love to hear from you.

Contact us

Email support@novawealthhq.com and we'll get back to you within 1–2 business days. Include your device model and a short description of what happened — screenshots help a lot.

Common questions

My reminders aren't firing.
Check Settings → Notifications → Daybook and make sure notifications are allowed. Reminders are scheduled on your device, so they need notification permission to appear.

I forgot my password.
On the sign-in screen, tap "Forgot password" — we'll email you a 6-digit code to set a new one.

How do I delete my account?
In the app: tap your avatar on the Today screen → Delete account. Deletion is immediate and permanent, and removes all of your data from our servers.

Is my data private?
Yes. No ads, no tracking, no analytics, no selling data. Read the full privacy policy.
